We are nonprofit team,
focusing on reducing poverty and social injustice.

Our NGO is directly benefitting over 15,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 400 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India.

Our approach is holistic and our style hands on. We are on the ground, where it matters most for the child. We enable both families and communities. We have several grass roots initiatives. Our Mission is to inspire and empower people to the culture of giving into changing the lives of the children in need. Our Vision is Reaching out to marginalized children in need, empowering them, bringing smiles and making them future ready.
